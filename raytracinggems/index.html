Ray Tracing Gems

A book about ray tracing edited by Eric Haines and Tomas Akenine-Möller, from Apress.

About

This is a book in development, with this page pointing at relevant resources. You can visit the Apress or Amazon pages for the book (note: the electronic version will be free). The hardcover is scheduled for mid-March, though we expect individual chapters to be available electronically before then. In the meantime, we've collected some ray tracing resources to tide you over.
